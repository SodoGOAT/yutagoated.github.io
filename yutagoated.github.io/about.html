About Yuta Okkotsu

Yuta Okkotsu is a key character in the anime and manga series "Jujutsu Kaisen". He is a special grade jujutsu sorcerer and one of the most powerful students at Tokyo Jujutsu High. Yuta's journey begins when he is cursed by his childhood friend Rika Orimoto, who becomes an immensely powerful vengeful spirit. With the help of his friends and mentors, Yuta learns to control Rika's power and use it for good.
