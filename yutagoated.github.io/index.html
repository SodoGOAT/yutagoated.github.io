About Yuta Okkotsu

Yuta Okkotsu is a special grade jujutsu sorcerer and a student at Tokyo Jujutsu High. Initially cursed by his childhood friend Rika Orimoto, Yuta learns to control her immense power and becomes a powerful sorcerer.
